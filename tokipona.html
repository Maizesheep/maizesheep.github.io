about me

what is toki pona?

toki pona is a language that is quite personal to me. It has been a big part of my conlanging journey and my foray into linguistics. It has also impacted my philosophy in a way, as I think since initially learning some toki pona, I've been better at simplifying my thoughts and breaking down my beliefs. I've also become less materialistic I feel, but that could be for other reasons.

Oh, and by the way the official website is tokipona.org

linja Mesipi lon lipu

I made a handwritten font with wasokeli's font maker! You can download it here: linja Mesipi lon lipu. I might update it in the future, maybe by adding long pi and other extendable glyphs (which the sp font maker currently does not support, but it can be added manually). Also, don't worry, it's under the OFL license, not "All rights reserved" or anything. I don't need anyone protecting the copyright on this.
