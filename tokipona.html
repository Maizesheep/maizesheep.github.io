what is toki pona?

toki pona is a language that is quite personal to me. It has been a big part of my conlanging journey and my foray into linguistics. It has also impacted my philosophy in a way, as I think since initially learning some toki pona, I've been better at simplifying my thoughts and breaking down my beliefs. I've also become less materialistic I feel, but that could be for other reasons.
